About

Education/Experience

Julian (he/him) initially followed an academic route that culminated in a PhD in physics (with a focus in biophysics and neuroscience), over a dozen peer-reviewed publications, and the founding of an AI art authentication company. Since taking the psilocybin facilitator training out of Oregon he has redirected his focus towards into one that puts somatic practices front and center, a lifelong personal pursuit. Bringing the same critical awareness and conscientious work ethic from academia, Julian now applies his skills to interpersonal, self-reflective, and somatic work. He is now equipped to cultivate meaningful connections and support others on journeys of self-discovery and personal growth, helping them tap into their process.
